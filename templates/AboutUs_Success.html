About the Meal Magic Team




The Meal Magic team - made up of Alex Goldman, Joel Reubenstein and Dan Haroun - has always been passionate about food. Living in a city like Philadelphia, where the number of incredible restaurants knows no bounds, they sometimes had trouble choosing a place to eat due to the overwhelming number of restaurant options. After putting a little thought into this problem, the idea for Meal Magic was born. This program makes it easy to discover new and delicious restaurants that people like you have enjoyed.

Try it out and let us know what you think!
